Reasonable questions,
asked often.

Will it sound like me, or like ChatGPT?

Rezumfit is trained to preserve voice. We treat your résumé as authoritative source material — we re-order, re-emphasise, and tighten, but we never invent experience or replace your phrasing with generic filler. If a sentence already sings, we leave it alone.

Is the model reading my résumé to train itself?

No. Your documents are processed in-session and never used as training data. Drafts are encrypted at rest and you can purge your library at any time from settings.

What about ATS — applicant tracking systems?

Every export passes a structural check: clean headings, parseable dates, no hidden tables. We score readability against the major ATS engines and flag anything brittle before you send.

Can I bring my own template?

Yes. Upload a DOCX or share a Figma file. Rezumfit fills your tailored draft into your existing layout — typography, hierarchy and all.

Does it write cover letters?

On the Tailor and Studio plans. Same philosophy: it studies the listing and your voice, then drafts something you'd actually have written on a good Tuesday.
