# RezumFit Business Strategy Pivot — Summary & Analysis

> Source: Business developer feedback to address zero sales since launch
> Date: March 2026

---

## Core Pivot Idea

Move from a **subscription-based "all-in-one" SaaS** to a **product-led, one-time payment with locked features and upsells**. Two distinct funnels based on user intent:

1. **Has a Resume** → ATS Checker funnel → Resume Optimizer
2. **No Resume** → Resume Builder funnel → Resume Builder package

Both end with the same in-app upsells (Job Scraper, Tracker, Interview Prep).

---

## Two Customer Journeys

### Journey 1: User WITH a Resume
1. Lands on **ATS Score landing page** (single CTA only — no other distractions)
2. Uploads resume → gets ATS score popup with improvement suggestions
3. Popup links to **Resume Optimizer Sales Page**
4. Picks 1 of 3 one-time payment packages (no monthly fees)
5. Gets app access — locked to what they paid for
6. **Bonus**: AI Cover Letter included
7. **Upsell inside app**: Job Scraper + Job Tracker + Interview Prep

### Journey 2: User WITHOUT a Resume
1. Lands on **Resume Builder landing page** (separate from ATS one)
2. Builds resume in the tool
3. Tries to download → blocked by popup → directed to **Resume Builder Sales Page**
4. Picks 1 of 3 one-time payment packages
5. Gets app access — locked to what they paid for
6. **Bonuses**: AI Cover Letter + LinkedIn Optimization + Interview Prep
7. **Upsell inside app**: Job Scraper + Job Tracker + Interview Prep

---

## Key Tactical Changes

| Area | Current | Proposed |
|------|---------|----------|
| **Pricing** | Subscription (Pro/Premium monthly + annual) | One-time payment, 3 tiers per product |
| **Landing pages** | One homepage with everything | Two separate funnels (ATS / Builder) |
| **Feature access** | Subscribers get everything | Hard-locked features by purchase |
| **Bonuses** | None bundled | Cover letter, LinkedIn, Interview Prep as bundle bait |
| **Exit intent** | None | Pop-up offering Telegram community |
| **Upsells** | None inside app | Job Scraper, Tracker, Interview Prep as in-app upsells |
| **Email** | Drip emails exist | Reminder + upsell sequences post-purchase |

## Implementation Checklist (per the doc)

1. **ATS Checker landing page** — single CTA, removes everything else
2. **Resume Builder landing page** — separate funnel for no-resume users
3. **Resume Optimizer sales page** — 3 packages, one-time payment
4. **Resume Builder sales page** — 3 packages with bigger bonus stack
5. **Exit-intent popups** — Telegram community offer
6. **In-app feature locks** — block unpaid features per package
7. **In-app upsell popups** — job scraper, tracker, interview prep
8. **Email sequences** — reminder + upsell flows
